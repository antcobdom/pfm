Connections

```
PIN     NRF24L01    RPI             RPi -P1 Connector
1       GND         rpi-gnd         (25)
2       VCC         rpi-3v3         (17)
3       CE          rpi-gpio22      (15)****
4       CSN         rpi-gpio8       (24)
5       SCK         rpi-sckl        (23)
6       MOSI        rpi-mosi        (19)
7       MISO        rpi-miso        (21)
8       IRQ         Not Connected
```
